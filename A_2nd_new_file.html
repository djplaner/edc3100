Important tasks you must complete before the end of Week 2 so that you can go on Professional Experience.

Overview

As you should know, this course includes an embedded Professional Experience (PE). You cannot pass this course, without passing your Professional Experience (PE). Assignment 3 is designed to help you prepare, implement, and reflect on your Professional Experience.

It is time that you make sure that you are prepared for Professional Experience.

If you are repeating this course within 12 months of your last attempt, you do not have to go on Professional Experience. Please ignore the following and read this.

What you have to do - NOW

Really, you should be doing the following steps NOW or at least really, really, soon.

  1. Check that your Blue Card (or equivalent) is current.
  2. Check the dates for Professional Experience.
  3. Submit a placement form to your nearest PE office.

What if you can't make these dates?

If you can't make these dates, then your options are:

  1. Apply for a variation in dates.

    Such an application will need to be made to the Professional Experience office. The approval process does required final approval from the Course Examiner.

    NOTE: Be sure you first read the section on variations below.

  2. Drop the course.

    Seriously, you can't pass the course without completing Professional Experience.

PE date variations

In EDC3100, the only variations to PE dates that are allowed are either starting:

  1. no more than a week before the normal dates; or,
  2. after the normal dates.

You will almost certainly NOT be allowed to start your Professional Experience early.

This is because you will need to integrate ICTs into your teaching on Professional Experience and the learning paths in Module 3 prepare you for this task. In addition, assignment 2 is due only a few weeks before hand. This means that it is unlikely that you will have engaged in the Module 3 learning paths effectively before going on PE.

Given that the explicit aim of your Professional Experience is to integrate the use of ICTs into your pedagogy, not completing the Module 3 learning paths is a serious issue. Hence early starts are usually not approved.

There is some significant flexibility around starting PE later. A later start on PE, also means that you will get an extension on Assignment 3 (including the learning journal).

Are you exempt from PE?

Please ignore this page if you will be going on PE for EDC3100.

If you are repeating this course within 12 months of failing it and you have previously passed the Professional Experience component, then you may not need to go on Professional Experience this semester.

If you are in this situation, then:

  1. Please contact the Professional Experience office ASAP to confirm that you are exempt.
  2. If exempt, please inform the Course Examiner - CE_NAME.

Assignment 3 is based around Professional Experience, if you are exempt you aren't going on PE, so what do you do? The learning paths from Module 3 will explain what you will need to do about Assignment 3.
